Anti Spam

As a business based in the EU, we're hugely pleased with the latest privacy rules and are committed to partnering with MIMO customers and users to help them understand and prepare for the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

As a provider of both automated marketing & WiFi services, preparing for GDPR has been a lengthy process. We've taken the opportunity to increase our security and migrate customer data to the EU. We rigorously test our systems for security vulnerabilities and use Cloudflare to protect our services from abuse. We use Google and Amazon's firewalls to secure our service.
